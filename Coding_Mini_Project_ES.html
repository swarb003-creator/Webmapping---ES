Duluth Points of Interest

by: Ella Swarbrick

Duluth, Minnesota which is located on the coast of Lake Superior is known for its picturesque hiking trails, great food, fun activities, and many other amazing attractions. While you are visiting Duluth for the 2026 FDI World Dental Congress make sure to check out these ten unique attractions Duluth has to offer! The attractions are split into different categories showcased by their map marker, and each marker will display a pop-up with information about the attraction and a link to their website when clicked on. We at Local Duluth Dentist Office hope this map helps you prepare for your upcoming visit and we are looking forward to this year's conference in our beautiful city.
